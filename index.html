about

I was a scientific collaborator in the Computational Linguistics group at the Computer science department of the Université de Neuchâtel. My PhD focused on the design, implementation and evaluation of text clustering algorithms based on stylistic features and was supervised by Prof. Jacques Savoy. The project "Clustering with Styles" is part of the Computational Linguistics and Stylometry Studies.

I got the Swiss Joint Master of Science in Computer Science (summa cum laude) of the Universities of Bern, Neuchâtel, and Fribourg, Switzerland. I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (cum laude) from the University of Bern, Switzerland.
